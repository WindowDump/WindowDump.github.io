Who is this person

My handle is Window Dump (check your man pages to learn more about me, I just output the content of a window to a file in text format). I like computers. I figured out how to program them and get them to do increasingly silly stuff - this blog and its content is just the latest example.

If you’re looking for my old fork of KeyZen, a tool used to practice keyboard layouts (i.e., Dvorak, Colemak, Hands Down), you can find it right here, in the usual location. Features sounds from some rectangle. I use Cornes btw.

Current fixations include (but are not limited to):

  • indie TTRPG’s / DIY elfgames
    • OSR / post-OSR / NSR / just OSR again
      • OSR stands for Original Sicilian Ravioli
    • story games
    • one-page games
    • games that dislike categorization
  • Typst, typography, graphic design
    • I had to brush up on webdev stuff for this site
  • retail WoW and classic hardcore WoW

I’m into rules-light games after early exposure to Risus proved fatal. Some of my favorite niches include the World of Dungeons extended family, the LUMEN extended family, Whitehack, Longshot City, Orbital Blues, Fabula Ultima, and Forged in the Dark. This list will change the next time I look at it.

I’m currently running Orbital Blues and maybe FIST or Grimwild. I’m playing The Wildsea. I recently finished a long game of Cyberpunk RED and might rant about it.
